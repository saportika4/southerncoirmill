About Us

FAQ

What products do you offer?
We offer a range of carpets, rugs, doormats, and handcrafts. Our products include coir carpets, coir rugs, cotton rugs, jute rugs, polyester rugs, viscose rugs and a range of doormats and handcrafts.
What materials are your products made from?
Our products are made from a variety of materials including coir, cotton, jute, polyester and viscose.
What is coir?
Coir is a natural fiber extracted from the husk of coconut and is used in various forms such as chips, fiber, and dust.
How are your coir products made?
Our coir products are made from the dust and fibers of the coconut husk. The husk is soaked in water to soften it and then beaten to separate the fibers. The fibers are then cleaned and spun into yarn which is used to make our coir products.
What are some benefits of using coir products?
Coir products are eco-friendly and sustainable alternatives to peat-based products for gardening and agriculture. They are free from soil-borne pathogens and weeds, so your plants are 100% protected and safe.
Are your products eco-friendly?
Yes! Our coir products are 100% natural and biodegradable.
Are your coir products safe for pets?
Yes! Our coir products are 100% natural and safe for pets.
Can I use your coir products outdoors?
Yes! Many of our coir products are suitable for outdoor use. Please refer to the product description for specific information.
How long will my coir product last?
Coir is a durable material and products made from it can last for several years. However, the lifespan of a coir product such as a carpet, rug or doormat can vary depending on factors such as usage and maintenance. Proper care and cleaning can help extend the life of your coir product.
Are your products handmade?
Many of our products are handmade by skilled artisans using traditional techniques.
What are some benefits of using sustainable products?
Using sustainable products helps combat climate change without sacrificing quality.
